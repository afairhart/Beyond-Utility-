Our Thesis

Water is entering its most transformative era

The systems that have delivered and treated water for over a century are reaching their limits. New demands from industry, agriculture, and growing populations require fundamentally different approaches.

We see a future of distributed treatment, intelligent water grids, industrial-scale reuse, and buildings that actively manage their own water. New business models and infrastructure are emerging to serve every person and every business in ways that centralized systems alone cannot.

Beyond Utility Water Ventures invests at the earliest stages in the companies making this future a reality. We target pre-seed and seed globally, focused on the areas we believe will see the fastest growth in the next decade.

Access to more water, like access to more energy, is foundational to human prosperity. Greater water access improves life expectancy, food supply, sanitation, and quality of life.

We believe the technologies enabling greater access and efficiency will define the next generation of water infrastructure.

Commercial & Residential Water Intelligence
