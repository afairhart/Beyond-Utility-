Key Assumptions & Methodology

Fund Structure

$12.5M target fund. 2% annual management fee over a 10-year fund life. 20% carried interest above a preferred return (not modeled in the base IRR).

Investment Strategy

Pre-seed checks into 10–20 water technology companies. ~50% of capital reserved for initial deployment, ~50% reserved for follow-on into the strongest performers.

Follow-On Approach

Up to 3–5 companies receive follow-on capital at the Seed or Series A stage (modeled at Year 2). Follow-on is reserved for companies demonstrating product-market fit and revenue traction.

Exit Assumptions

All exits modeled at a single exit year (default Year 10) for simplicity. In practice, exits will be staggered via M&A, secondary sales, or IPO across years 5–12.

Outcome Distribution

Default multiples follow a power-law pattern: a few outsized winners (25–40x), a tier of strong performers (10–15x), and a tail of moderate-to-low returns. Drag the chart to model alternative scenarios.

IRR Methodology

Net IRR is computed using Newton-Raphson with bisection fallback over annual cashflows. Fee drag is included as annual cash outflows. The IRR reflects the LP's time-value-adjusted return.
