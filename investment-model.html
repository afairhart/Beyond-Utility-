Key Assumptions & Methodology

Fund Structure

$12.5M target fund. 2% annual management fee over a 10-year fund life, resulting in $2.5M in fees and $10M investable capital. 20% carried interest above a preferred return (carry not modeled in base IRR).

Investment Strategy

Pre-seed checks of $400k into 10–20 water technology companies. 50% of investable capital allocated to initial checks, 50% reserved for follow-on into the strongest performers.

Follow-On Approach

Up to 3 companies receive significant follow-on capital at the Seed or Series A stage (modeled at Year 2). Follow-on concentration reflects higher valuations at later stages—fewer bets, larger checks into proven winners.

Exit Assumptions

All exits modeled at Year 10 for simplicity. In practice, exits will be staggered across years 5–12 via M&A, secondary sales, or IPO.

Outcome Distribution

Default multiples follow a power-law distribution: a few outsized winners (25–40x), a tier of strong performers (10–15x), and a tail of moderate-to-low returns. Drag the chart or edit deal multiples to model alternative scenarios.

IRR Methodology

Net IRR computed via Newton-Raphson with bisection fallback over annual cashflows. Management fee drag is included as annual outflows. Reflects the LP’s time-value-adjusted net return over the fund life.
